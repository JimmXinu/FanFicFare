Few things to know, which will make your life substantially easier:
  1. Small post written by me — how to read fiction in Stanza or any other ebook reader.
  2. Currently we support fanfiction.net, fictionpress.com, fanficauthors.net and ficwad.com
  3. Paste a URL of the first chapter of the fanfic, not the index page
  4. Fics with a single chapter are not supported (you can just copy and paste it)
  5. Stories which are too long may not be downloaded correctly and application will report a time-out error — this is a limitation which is currently imposed by Google AppEngine on a long-running activities
  6. FicWad support is somewhat flaky — if you feel it doesn't work for you, send all the details to me
  7. You can download fanfics and store them for 'later' by just downloading them and visiting recent downloads section, but in future they will be deleted after 5 days to save the space
  8. If Downloader simply opens a download file window rather than saves the fanfic and gives you a link, it means it is too large to save in the database and you need to download it straight away
  9. If you think that something that should work in fact doesn't, drop me a mail to sigizmund@gmail.com
Otherwise, just have fun, and if you want to say thank you — use the email above.
